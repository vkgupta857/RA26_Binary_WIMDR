Welcome to Geo Tracking Software

This is an AI based drone software for geo tracking of wastes. With the availability of drone the municipal coorporation can keep track of waste and get automatic generated report and implement waste management within 24 hours.

Our smart approach
  • Detect if there is waste in the image and classify if its high waste, medium waste or low waste.
  • Tag the image with its location and update the database with waste quality, location, date and timing of the image.
  • Send an alert to the nearest waste management Government officer for quick action.
  • Create a map visualization for detecting areas with high waste. The visualisation varied with time would also be provided.
  • Provide statistical graphs depicting waste management reports over the week.
